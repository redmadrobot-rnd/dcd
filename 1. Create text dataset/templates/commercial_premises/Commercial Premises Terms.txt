### 4. Signage and Branding

**Signage policy:** {{commercial_signage_policy}}. Tenants must submit signage plans for approval before installation. Signage design, size, and placement are regulated to maintain a consistent appearance. Illuminated signs may require additional approval and compliance with municipal regulations.

### 5. Access and Operating Hours

Commercial premises typically have independent street or lobby access to allow operation outside residential hours. Tenants are responsible for their own security systems within their lot. Operating hours may be subject to restrictions to minimise disturbance to residents; such restrictions are usually set out in the lease or house rules.

### 6. Assignment and Subletting

Tenants wishing to assign the lease or sublet the premises usually require the landlord's prior written consent. The landlord may conduct due diligence on the proposed assignee or subtenant. Assignment or subletting without consent may constitute a breach of the lease.
