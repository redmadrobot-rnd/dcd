### 3. Services Included

The tariff typically covers common area maintenance, waste removal, and basic building services. This may include cleaning of lobbies, stairwells, and corridors; maintenance of lifts and entrance doors; landscaping of the courtyard; and operation of security and access control systems. ### 4. Payment and Billing

Residents receive a monthly or quarterly invoice for maintenance ({{maintenance_tariff}} RUB/sqm) and utilities. Payment can usually be made via bank transfer, online banking, or through a resident portal. Late payment may incur penalties as specified in the management contract. Residents are advised to keep records of payments for their own reference and for dispute resolution if needed. Contact {{management_company}} for payment-related inquiries.

### 5. Tariff Indexation and Changes

The maintenance tariff (currently {{maintenance_tariff}} RUB/sqm per month) may be adjusted annually in line with inflation, changes in utility costs, or decisions of the homeowners' association. Residents are entitled to advance notice of any tariff increase, typically at least one month before it takes effect. {{management_company}} must provide justification for increases and, where required by law, obtain approval from the general meeting of owners.

### 6. Dispute Resolution

If a resident disagrees with an invoice, the quality of services, or a decision of the management company, they may raise the matter in writing. The management company is obliged to respond within a specified period. If the dispute cannot be resolved, the resident may refer the matter to the homeowners' association, a housing dispute resolution body, or the courts as appropriate under local law.
